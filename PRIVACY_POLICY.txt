PRIVACY POLICY FOR WHISP

Last Updated: November 17, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how RTechnologies ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Whisp mobile application (the "App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Account Information
When you create an account with Whisp, we collect the following information:
- Full name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted and stored securely)
- Profile picture or avatar
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Country of residence
- Authentication tokens for session management

1.2 Authentication Information
- If you sign up using email and password, we collect and store your email and encrypted password
- If you sign up using Google Sign In, we collect your Google account information (name, email, profile picture) as provided by Google

1.3 Communication Data
- Messages, text, images, audio recordings, and other content you send or receive through the App
- Chat history and conversation data
- Friend connections and social interactions

1.4 Device and Technical Information
- Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- IP address
- App usage data and analytics
- Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications
- Network information

1.5 Media and Content
- Photos and images you upload or share through the App
- Audio recordings (voice messages) you create and share
- Any other media files you choose to share

1.6 Payment Information
- If you purchase premium features, payment information is processed through Stripe
- We do not store your full payment card details; payment processing is handled securely by Stripe
- We may collect billing information such as transaction history and subscription status

1.7 Advertising Data
- Google Mobile Ads may collect information for personalized advertising
- Ad interaction data and analytics

2.1 To Provide and Maintain Our Service

- Deliver push notifications
- Process premium subscription payments

2.2 To Improve Our Service
- Analyze app usage and performance
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features and functionality
- Personalize your experience

2.3 To Communicate With You
- Send you push notifications about messages, friend requests, and app updates
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send important service-related announcements

2.4 For Security and Safety
- Authenticate your identity
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
- Enforce our terms of service
- Protect the rights and safety of our users

2.5 For Advertising
- Display personalized advertisements through Google Mobile Ads
- Measure ad performance and effectiveness

4.1 Data Storage

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Secure password hashing and storage

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
